Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites may take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications with databases can take 2-4 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

What's your development process?

I follow a structured process: 1) Consultation to understand your needs, 2) Planning and proposal, 3) Development with regular updates, 4) Testing and refinement, 5) Deployment and ongoing support.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer various support packages after project completion. This includes bug fixes, updates, feature additions, and performance monitoring to ensure your solution continues to meet your needs.

What technologies do you work with?

I work with a wide range of technologies including HTML/CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Python, React, Java, C, C++, and various data science and machine learning libraries.

How do you handle project communication?

I maintain regular communication through your preferred channels (email, phone, video calls). You'll receive weekly progress updates and have opportunities to provide feedback at each development stage.

What industries have you worked with?

I've worked with clients across various industries including education, healthcare, e-commerce, finance, and non-profit organizations. My diverse skill set allows me to adapt to different business needs.
